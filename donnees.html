DONNÉES PERSONNELLES
[Edition - 2024]

Landscape edition, 7 x 14 cm format, bound with rings, printed on 120g paper and tracing paper.

Données personnelles takes us on an investigation into the heart of the digital world.

The edition traces the lives of two people using only their digital personal data. Though these two characters are different, they are led to the same outcome: committing the unthinkable. Données personnelles shows how the Internet can drive us toward uniform thinking and fuel hatred, sometimes leading to deviant behavior.
