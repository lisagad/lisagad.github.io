PLAGE GALACTIQUE

[A Beach in the Galaxy]

Creation of a comic strip entirely in Photoshop. This strip tells a short story and establishes a visual universe. The strip narrates the story of an astronaut landing on a new planet with unknown creatures similar to humans.

[Design & Creation]

All elements were created in Photoshop. For this comic strip, I wanted to give it a collage look to fully exploit the fictional aspect of this universe. The comic strip is in A4 format.
