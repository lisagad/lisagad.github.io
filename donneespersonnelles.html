DONNÉES PERSONNELLES

[Digital Survey]

Creation of an edition solely based on screenshots. The edition retraces the lives of two people using only their digital personal data. These two different characters will be led to the same end: committing the irreparable. "Personal Data" shows how the internet can lead us into a uniform way of thinking and fuel hatred, sometimes leading to deviant behaviors.

[Design & Creation]

Edition in 7 x 14 cm landscape format, bound with rings, printed on 120g paper and tracing paper.
