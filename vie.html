VIE CITADINE
[Sketchbook - 2023_24]

Vie Citadine is a series of two small A6 sketchbooks, bringing together sketches inspired by public transportation and urban advertisements.

Through these drawings, the idea is to depict elements of daily life, emphasizing often-overlooked details. Each sketch is an opportunity to experiment with different tools and techniques to represent them.
