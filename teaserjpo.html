TEASER JPO

[Teaser for the DN MADE Augmented Graphics Program]

Transform the printed poster into a teaser to present and highlight the DN MADE Augmented Graphics program at Lycée Jacques Prévert. The teaser takes the form of a title sequence disrupted by the visual elements of the poster.

[Design & Creation]

The teaser is entirely made in After Effects. It is in 1920 x 1080 pixels format and lasts 30 seconds. It is designed for landscape format screens only.
