COUVERTURES FICTIVES

[Magazine Covers]

This project is a fictional commission for the magazines Les Inrockuptibles and Rolling Stone. Each photo needed to convey a particular ambiance that matched the magazine's universe.

[Design & Creation]

The objective was to photograph the same person and place them in different ambiances to fit each magazine's universe. After taking the photos, they were retouched and placed on the covers. The covers are in A4 format.
