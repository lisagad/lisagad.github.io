INAUGURATION DU THÉÂTRE-CINÉMA
[Communication - 2024]

Landscape-format A1 panels were created for the Fontenay-le-Fleury theater-cinema, under the direction of the City Hall.

For its reopening after several months of renovation, the building’s inauguration took place on September 13, 2024. To highlight the work accomplished and those involved in the project, explanatory panels were created.

These panels adopt the colors of the theater-cinema, adhering to its graphic guidelines. A visual rhythm is established through variations in size and graphic elements, allowing for a clear hierarchy of information. The panels blend harmoniously into the surrounding space.

[With the participation of…]
Céline Henriques – Deputy General Director & Director of Communication and Culture at Fontenay-le-Fleury City Hall
