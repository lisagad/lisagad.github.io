GYMNASIUM RULES
[Signage / communication - 2024]

These A4 posters are designed for the gyms of Fontenay-le-Fleury, under the supervision of the Fontenay-le-Fleury Town Hall.

The gym rules have been rewritten in a more positive style, accessible to all ages. New rules, new design! This project proposes a more colorful and dynamic visual, with a play on typography sizes to modernize the rules posters present in the gyms. The aim is to make this document, often perceived as restrictive, more attractive and meaningful for users.

[With the participation of...]
Céline Henriques - DGA & Director of Communications and Culture, Fontenay-le-Fleury Town Hall
