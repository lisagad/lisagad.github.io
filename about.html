People call me Lisa, Lise, Lisou… but my full name is Lisa Gadeaud! I’m a student in graphic design and digital media in Boulogne-Billancourt.

I love experimenting, trying new ways to create, and working with different media. Versatility is key for me. I’m curious and always eager to learn and grow.

I’m also someone who loves to have fun and keeps an open mind!

On the side, I practice photography, both digital and film. I enjoy every moment and pay attention to each detail. I aim to bring out this sensitivity and fleeting quality in my work. A romantic at heart, I also love reading, writing, and diving into art.

My world draws inspiration from Gothic architecture, Chilla’s Art games, Depeche Mode, and the Dada movement.

Feel free to reach out if you're curious to see what a project with me could look like! ☺
