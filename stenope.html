STÉNOPÉ
[Experimentation – 2024]

This short series explores the use of the pinhole camera, a lensless photographic device. This project gave me the opportunity to train in photo development in the darkroom.

[DEFINITION]
A pinhole is a small hole in a barrier, used as a photographic lens to project an image onto a photosensitive surface.
